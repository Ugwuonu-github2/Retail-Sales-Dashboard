# 📊 Retail Sales Dashboard

An end-to-end **Business Intelligence** project that analyzes retail sales data using **SQL (DuckDB)** and **Microsoft Excel** to transform raw data into actionable business insights. The project demonstrates data cleaning, business metric calculation, dashboard development, and executive reporting.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project simulates the role of a Business Intelligence Analyst tasked with helping a retail company monitor its sales performance and support data-driven decision-making.

The project follows a complete BI workflow:

- Data Collection
- Data Cleaning & Preparation
- SQL Business Analysis
- KPI Calculation
- Dashboard Development
- Business Insight Generation
- Executive Reporting

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large amounts of transactional data every day. Without proper analysis, it is difficult to identify trends, monitor performance, understand customer behavior, and make informed business decisions.

This dashboard provides an executive overview of sales performance by answering key business questions related to revenue, profitability, customers, products, and regional performance.

# 📌 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, management should consider:

- Investing more in high-performing product categories.
- Increasing inventory for top-selling products.
- Developing marketing campaigns for underperforming regions.
